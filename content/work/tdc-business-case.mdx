## Context

TDC Summit Fest 2024 Business Case Competition (team Idaman Mertua). Case: sustainability strategy for Bobobox in Indonesian hospitality.

## Problem

How Bobobox can communicate and execute sustainability (e.g. carbon-emission reduction ambition and related initiatives) while remaining competitive over a multi-year horizon.

## Role

Team member contributing structured case analysis and recommendation design. Placement verified via personal 1st-place certificate (Sebastian Salutare Noel Lumme).

## Approach

- Situation–complication–question framing and executive summary
- Market and emissions background with public industry context
- Problem analysis across policy, industry structure, and consumer drivers
- ACR strategy framework (Action / Collaboration / Revolution) as the recommendation spine

## Constraint

Competition deliverable for a branded case company. Recommendations are proposals — **not** implemented corporate strategy or claimed client results.

## Result

**1st place**, TDC Business Case Competition, TDC Summit Fest 2024 (Surabaya, 30 Nov 2024). Deliverable: strategy pitch deck (ACR Strategy).
