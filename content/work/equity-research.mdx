## Context

Equity research internship at Henan Putihrai Sekuritas (Jan–Jun 2026), supporting the research division on Indonesia-listed equities and daily market reporting.

## Problem

Coverage teams need repeatable analysis: company and industry context, financial statements, and a clear investment view under time pressure — not one-off decks.

## Role

Supported the research process end-to-end: disclosures and market data → structured analysis → memo-ready framing. Individual contribution under research-division supervision; not sole coverage ownership.

## Approach

- Business-model and financial-statement analysis across **15+ IDX-listed companies** in **5 sectors**
- Financial models, forecasts, and valuation framing for investment memos
- **100+ daily market reports** synthesizing macro, sector, and company developments

## Constraint

Public markets material and firm process standards; no claim of personal P&amp;L or audited investment performance.

## Result

A defensible research workflow: company analysis, modeling, and memo preparation that supported institutional reporting cadence. Scale figures match career timeline (verified in-repo).
